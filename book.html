Paul Rand Essay Book Design

Book composition design of Paul Rand’s essay, “Design and the Play Instinct”.

Role

  • Designer

Tools Used

  • Adoble InDesign
  • Adoble Illustrator

O V E R V I E W

Challenge

The challenge of this assignment was to create a book composition that embodies Paul Rand’s idea of design and play. This book contains the text of his essay and images that are up to us to place and bring together.
